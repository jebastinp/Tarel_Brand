About Tarel

Fresh, honest food — from trusted source to your kitchen.

At Tarel, we believe everyone deserves fresh, high-quality seafood and meat delivered with honesty and care. From trusted farms and fisheries to your kitchen, every product is selected, packed and delivered under strict quality standards.

Our speciality is authentic Indian fish varieties that are hard to find in the UK — brought to Scotland fresh, every week. Whether you're cooking at home or buying for your restaurant, Tarel is committed to freshness, consistency and authentic taste.

  • Authentic Indian fish, sourced with care
  • Strict cold-chain from source to doorstep
  • Trusted by homes, restaurants & retailers
